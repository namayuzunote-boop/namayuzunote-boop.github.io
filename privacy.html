Privacy Policy — JobTally

Effective Date: May 4, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use JobTally ("the App").

Data Storage

All data you enter in JobTally is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external server. This includes:

  • Job records, work logs, and material lists
  • Business profile information (name, address, contact details, tax numbers)
  • Generated estimates and invoices
  • Site photos attached to jobs

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain device information to serve relevant ads. This may include:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • General location information

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

No Account Required

JobTally does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the App.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into the App is Google AdMob for advertising. No other analytics, tracking, or data-sharing services are used.
